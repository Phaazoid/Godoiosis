# Aeroport

🟡 **Draft canon** · `Story/Locations/CPF/Notable Places/Aeroport.docx`
**Type:** city ([CPF](cpf.md)) · one end of the [Silk Road](silk-road.md)

A city of **tall erected platforms** spanning a canyon, built up over time to support airship traffic. Its layout is **haphazard and confusing**. Hundreds of docking stations let airships set up shop or just refuel and move on; a network of platforms links the docking bays to a **large central platform** crowded with permanent merchant shops, tourists, and frequent **robbery**.

Beneath the city runs a maze of **maintenance pathways**, snaked through the architecture like an anthill, some reaching all the way to the ground. Many are maintained — but obsolete ones are abandoned and become perfect spots for **shady deals** or for a lost tourist to "disappear forever." It sits **deep in mechanist country** at one end of the major trade road; you can only reach it by **airship**. The only permanent residents live on the docked ships and in the central-platform shops.

The city has **two major sections** — one at the base of the plateau, one at the top. Long ago they were **two separate cities**, the journey around the plateau too long and dangerous for trade. Over time (perhaps with the help of **ancient alchemy** — a good example of alchemy literally reshaping the map) a path was carved up the **sheer plateau face**, with livable infrastructure built along it, fusing the two into one. Today it reads as a single city, but as you move **lower or higher** you can still feel the **cultural shifts** that trace back to its split origins.

> **Battle hook:** flagged as a **Cartel** location in [battle-locales.md](battle-locales.md). Verticality, choke-points, and "push enemies off the side" play (air alchemists / mace-users) all suit the platform-and-canyon geography.
