# The Outskirts

🟡 **Draft canon** · `Story/Locations/Other Notable Locations/The Outskirts.docx`
**Type:** region (border lands)

Near the borders the population thins, and cities and towns give way to **villages and settlements**. Politics and law mean less out here; people get by on **small-town values**. Recently, rumors of **marauding bandits and "mercenaries"** ravaging local populations have prompted both nations to move more armed force into the area — hence the **alchemy encampment the game starts near.**

This is the Bleeding Hearts' home turf and the staging ground for Part 1. It's also where **[the Cartel](../world/factions.md#the-cartel)** does its quiet predation — the outskirts are where people "made alone by hardship" disappear, and where the Cartel's **underground favela** hides.

> Related: [the Wilderness](wilderness.md) lies beyond the settled border; the Cartel's favela is an outskirts-of-Paracelsus location.
