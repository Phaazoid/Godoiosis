# The Great Scrap Heaps

🟡 **Draft canon** · `Story/Locations/CPF/Notable Places/The Great Scrap Heaps.docx`
**Type:** region ([CPF](cpf.md))

The CPF's vast **industrial-waste dumping grounds** — toxic in places, but so enormous they've become a **landmark**. There are literally tons of waste here, continuously **scavenged by the country's poor**: you can find a chainsword — or even an entire **airship** — built almost entirely from salvaged parts. Some scavengers lean on their own (or others') weaker alchemy to help, some purely on technical skill; either way, they **survive off the industrial waste of the big cities**.

See [cpf.md § Why there's so much to scavenge](cpf.md#why-theres-so-much-to-scavenge-backstory) for how the heaps came to be and the scavenger economy that loops salvage back into city shops.

> **Battle hook:** a "mechanist slums / scrap" environment — improvised cover, salvage, and a stark class-contrast backdrop. See [battle-locales.md](battle-locales.md).
