# The Wilderness

🟡 **Draft canon (open)** · `Story/Locations/Other Notable Locations/The Wilderness.docx`
**Type:** region (unsettled)

The **unsettled** majority of the landmass — mountain ranges and large, poorly-documented forests. **Alchemists consider themselves better equipped to venture here** thanks to their command of the elements. Somewhere in the mountains is the **dragons' adopted natural habitat.**

Story roles the source floats for it:
- The site of **terrible aether-alchemy experimentation** — twisted creatures, the **secret dragon labs** (run in Part 3 by [Torv's Sister](../characters/npcs/torvs-sister.md); the place that took [Torv](../characters/torv.md)'s leg).
- A candidate location for the **[Philosopher's Keep](philosophers-keep.md)**.
- A place the airship could go down; a region to traverse mid-game.

Open worldbuilding questions it raises (kept deliberately): *How much of this world do humans really know? How far has anyone explored? Are there civilizations beyond the two great nations — indigenous peoples living in the wilderness?*

> The wilderness is also the thematic home of the **"absolute power / sealed cursed places"** idea — the dragon labs reframed as the site of an **ancient alchemical disaster** still physically anomalous today. See [themes.md § Absolute power corrupts](../themes.md#absolute-power-corrupts-absolutely-the-philosophers-arc) and [Q2](../appendix/open-questions.md#q2-the-philosophers-power-and-goal).
