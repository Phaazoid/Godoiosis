# Marco

🟡 **Draft canon** · `Story/Characters/Playable/Marco.docx`
**Role:** alchemist · **Weapon:** **hydraulic bionic arm** (water-enhanced) · **Affinity:** water

An easy-going male **water alchemist** — tolerant and a good listener. He has a **hydraulic bionic arm** that he enhances with water alchemy in battle. He's the de facto **confidant** of the group: he seldom pushes his own opinions, but makes insightful observations and lets others reach the conclusions themselves.

Marco survived a **famine that destroyed his family**, then **deliberately got himself imprisoned** because at least prison had food. When [Torv](torv.md)'s group stormed the prison to free one of their own, Marco escaped too — but **lost his arm** in the chaos. For that reason he feels little cause to complain as long as he has friends, food, and purpose. He is **very loyal to Torv**.

**If Marco had the Philosopher's Stone:** he would **give it to Torv**, whom he believes wiser than himself.
