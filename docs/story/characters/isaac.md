# Isaac

🟡 **Draft canon** · `Story/Characters/Playable/Isaac.docx`
**Role:** protagonist / alchemist · **Affinity:** **alkahest** (can use any primary rune without gaining an affinity for it) · **Family:** brother of [Rebecca](rebecca.md)

The main alchemist character. His unique **alkahest** affinity lets him use any primary rune without ever gaining a specific affinity — making him extraordinarily versatile, able to wield far more runes than anyone else, and the **key to recreating the Philosopher's Stone** (see [alchemy-lore.md](../world/alchemy-lore.md)).

Isaac is loyal and smart, and will do anything for his sister, Rebecca. He can be **overprotective**, though — a subconscious reaction to the death of their parents — which makes their relationship rocky at times, even as she knows he's only looking out for her.

Until the academy's affinity test, Isaac believed he had no affinity at all: his parents had always insisted on it. The discovery blindsides him and leaves him feeling **betrayed** — why did they hide this, and why were they so adamant? (The answer: [Dead Parents](npcs/dead-parents.md).)
