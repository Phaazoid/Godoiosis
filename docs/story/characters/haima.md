# Haima

🟡 **Draft canon** · `Story/Characters/Playable/Haima.docx`
**Role:** alchemist · **Affinity:** air

A female **air alchemist** — not strong, but **fast and agile**. She's playful and boisterous, gets along well with others (when she isn't pranking them), and has a **crush on [Aldin](aldin.md)**, who is oblivious to it. As the stakes escalate she turns **more sentimental than jolly**, feeling the horrors of war against the tight bonds of the group.

**If Haima had the Philosopher's Stone:** she would use it to stay **perpetually young.**
