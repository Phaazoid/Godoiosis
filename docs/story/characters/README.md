## Humors

The elemental temperament quick-keys (thematic frame: [themes.md § Humorism](../themes.md#humorism--a-character-design-tool-not-plot); compare the ⚪ [Myers-Briggs relic](../appendix/myers-briggs.md)). An alchemist's humor follows their affinity; the cast predates the scheme but mostly agrees with it (Ali reads choleric, Marco phlegmatic). Assigning humors to the non-alchemists — by temperament alone, no element to derive from — is an open pass.

| Element | Humor (fluid) | Temperament |
|---|---|---|
| Fire | yellow bile | **choleric** — irritable, quick to anger |
| Water | phlegm | **phlegmatic** — calm, unemotional even in danger |
| Earth | black bile | **melancholic** — sad, gives up easily |
| Air | blood | **sanguine** — happy, joking, confident in danger |
| Aether | ichor | **ichoric** — vivid, wonder-struck, drawn to living things; gives too much of themselves |

- **Ichor** (adopted 2026-07-13) is the fluid in the veins of the gods — and, in Hippocratic medicine, the serum that weeps from wounds — to blood what Aether is to the other four elements. An ichoric excess is an excess of *life*: warm, radiant, a little uncanny, prone to spending itself on others. [Aldin](aldin.md) is the type specimen.
- A sixth **alkahestic** humor (an excess of the *unformed* — personality as a decision, not an inheritance) was sketched and **shelved 2026-07-13**: alkahest never displays in game, and no system should hang off it. If it ever returns it's story texture for [Isaac](isaac.md), not a mechanic.
