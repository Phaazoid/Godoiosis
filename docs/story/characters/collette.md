# Collette

🟡 **Draft canon** · `Story/Characters/Playable/Collete.docx` *(filename "Collete"; body spells it **Collette**)*
**Role:** mechanist · **Weapon:** springspear

A **shy** female mechanist who wields a **springspear** — skilled and fast, but not very strong. She stays reticent and calm **until one of her friends is hurt, whereupon she goes berserk.** Close friends with [Haima](haima.md).

Collette was born to a young unwed mother working a factory for a meager wage. She joined the workforce young to earn extra, but as labor conditions worsened she grew **rebellious and ultimately fled** — and feels **guilty for leaving her mother behind**, a past she doesn't like to discuss. She trains hard but quietly, hoping one day to **free her mother** and provide for her with her share of the bounty.

**If Collette had the Philosopher's Stone:** she would transmute enough **gold** to pay off her mother's debt and buy a plot of land in the distant countryside where they could live together.
