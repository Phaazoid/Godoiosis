# Ross

🟡 **Draft canon** · `Story/Characters/Playable/Ross.docx`
**Role:** mechanist · **Weapon:** drills

A bulky male mechanist who fights with **drills**. Ross speaks his mind and has a **crude sense of humor** — likable enough, as long as you're on his side of a fight. Raised in the mechanist slums, he has an **underdeveloped sense of morality but a strong sense of loyalty**, thinking in terms of streetwise practicality over moral principle. He doesn't enjoy killing, but he enjoys **winning** — and in the game of battle, you do what you have to.

He has a **hidden softer side**: he secretly sends part of his loot to pay for the education of a young girl he rescued from a child-labor plant. His eagerness to get paid reads as greed, but he's really covering both himself and the girl.

**If Ross had the Philosopher's Stone:** he'd have no idea what to do with it and would **pawn it on the black market** for cash. (But first he'd say: *"I can dig it."*)
