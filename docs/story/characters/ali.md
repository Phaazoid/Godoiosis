# Ali

🟡 **Draft canon** · `Story/Characters/Playable/Ali.docx`
**Role:** alchemist · **Affinity:** fire · **Origin:** the [Sultanate](../world/factions.md#the-sultanate)

A dark-skinned male **fire alchemist** from a faraway nation who fancies himself **[Torv](torv.md)'s lieutenant**. He trains hard and pushes everyone else to do the same. He carries pride for his nation's unique alchemy but is open-minded about Paracelsian alchemy, using **fire runes** in battle.

His past is mysterious — rumor says he's **wanted for attempting to assassinate a sultan** back home.

**If Ali had the Philosopher's Stone:** he would transmute enough gold to retire to a **tropical island** and write his memoirs, embellished in purple prose.
