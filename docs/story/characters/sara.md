# Sara

🟡 **Draft canon** · `Story/Characters/Playable/Sara.docx`
**Role:** mechanist · **Weapon:** carbine · **Origin:** the [Sultanate](../world/factions.md#the-sultanate) · **Married to** [Said](said.md)

The wife of **[Said](said.md)**, who also uses **carbines** — **faster and more accurate** than he is, but hitting less hard. Like her husband she craves adventure, but is generally **more pragmatic and sensible** about it. She's **witty and proud**, always with a retort ready. She loves money, but loves Said more — though her affection comes out as **dizzying insults**.

**If Sara had the Philosopher's Stone:** she would **split it with her husband** and have them both live forever.
