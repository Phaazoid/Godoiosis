# Rebecca

🟡 **Draft canon** · `Story/Characters/Playable/Rebecca.docx`
**Role:** mechanist fighter · **Weapon:** **prosthetic combat arm** (upgradable); later broader mechanist weaponry · **Affinity:** none · **Family:** sister of [Isaac](isaac.md)

Isaac's sister, born with **no alchemic affinity**. After losing an arm in the raid on the Paracelsus Military Academy, she's fitted with a **prosthetic arm** that she fights with — eventually becoming well-versed in mechanist weaponry, though she primarily **upgrades her bionic arm** to grow in combat.

Rebecca is **ambitious yet naive**, having clung for years to the hope that she had an affinity. Let down by the test, she resolves to train at the Academy as an **officer** instead, wanting to learn all she can about alchemy regardless. She's composed under danger but takes no joy in fighting. Her *philosophy* (not her personality) is **Stoic**: rather than rage at what she can't control, she outwardly accepts it and moves on — which is how she meets the loss of her arm.

**If Rebecca had the Philosopher's Stone:** she would use it to **gain an alchemic affinity.**
