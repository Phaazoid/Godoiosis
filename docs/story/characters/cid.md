# Cid

🟡 **Draft canon** · `Story/Characters/Playable/Cid.docx` *(sparse source — mostly direction, not finished bio)*
**Role:** playable ally (joins after his rescue) · **Identity:** Prince of the [CPF](../world/factions.md#coalition-pax-facticius-cpf), son of [King Campeador](npcs/king-campeador.md)

Prince **Cid** of the CPF. The Bleeding Hearts rescue him from a kidnapping (Part 1) — unaware the kidnapping was staged by his **own father** to manufacture a pretext for war — and return him to the capital, after which he grows close to the group and **stows away** on the doomed airship.

Characterization so far (still a sketch):
- A **Zuko-like arc** — he starts out loyally serving his nation, then comes to realize his father is the villain.
- **Oblivious** to the King's plan to start a war.
- Personality: **diplomatic but somewhat condescending.**

> Resolved note: although filed under "Playable," Cid is also referenced as an NPC in the plot docs. He is **obtained as a playable ally** ("~Cid obtained~") while [King Campeador](npcs/king-campeador.md) remains a pure NPC. This page is the playable-ally bio; his father's scheming is covered on the King's page.
