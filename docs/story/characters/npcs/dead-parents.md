# Isaac & Rebecca's Parents

🟡 **Draft canon** · `Story/Characters/NPCs/Dead Parents.docx`
**Role:** backstory (deceased before the game) · **Family:** parents of [Isaac](../isaac.md) and [Rebecca](../rebecca.md)

The hidden root of Isaac's affinity and the siblings' poverty.

The **mother had an alkahest affinity** and had been **assaulted by assassins** in the past (the [Philosopher's Acolytes](../../world/factions.md#the-philosophers-acolytes) hunt alkahest bloodlines — see [timeline.md](../../timeline.md)). To stay hidden, the parents **moved to the borders and lived in poverty, without alchemy**, hiding their ability from their children and **lying that none of them had affinities** — which is why Isaac's academy test blindsides him.

They were happy despite the hardship — but were **eventually tracked down and killed**. Isaac and Rebecca, around **9–10 years old**, escaped and were forced to fend for themselves. The trauma shaped them both: **Isaac became overprotective**, **Rebecca a bit defiant** (and set on becoming an alchemist to escape poverty). Ten years later this leads to Rebecca's possession of a rune and Isaac's discovery of his own latent power.

> 🔴 A recurring "what if" across the docs: did the **Philosopher** spare the mother's line — perhaps unable to bring himself to kill a pregnant relative, and hiding it from his own cult — making **Isaac the deliberately-spared last alkahest bloodline**? → [Q2](../../appendix/open-questions.md#q2-the-philosophers-power-and-goal). The party is written to **learn what happened to the parents during Part 2** (the Cartel hunt), turning it personal.
