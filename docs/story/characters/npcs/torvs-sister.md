# Torv's Sister

🟡 **Draft canon (sketch)** · `Story/Characters/NPCs/Torv's Sister.docx` · *(barely sketched — almost all open)*
**Role:** Part 3 antagonist · **Affiliation:** high-class [Paracelsus](../../world/factions.md#paracelsus) — **not** Ouroboros · **Family:** sister of [Torv](../torv.md)

The Part-3 antagonist and **the first foe to make [Torv](../torv.md) falter.** What the source establishes (mostly as questions):

- **High Paracelsus, but not Ouroboros** — just powerful, well-placed elite.
- **Runs one of the secret dragon-training/experimentation facilities** in the wilderness ([locations/wilderness.md](../../locations/wilderness.md)) — making her the boss of the **Part 3 dragon battle**.
- 🔴 May have **forced Torv out of the wilderness** years ago — possibly the one who **took his leg** in an attempt to kill him, the unspoken reason he won't return. → see [Torv § Backstory](../torv.md#backstory).

Suggested motive (from the dragon-lab discussion): she may **spearhead a breakaway group of Paracelsus scientists** who question the official history of alchemy / the alchemist oligarchy — and she **pushed Torv away in the past to protect him**, viewing him as weak and not wanting him caught in the fallout of the shady things she was getting into. (And Ouroboros would almost certainly have **infiltrated** any such group.)

> Everything here is provisional; she's one of the least-developed major figures. Needs a name and a defined arc.
