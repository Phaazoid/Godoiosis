# The Cartel Leader

🟡 **Draft canon** · `Story/Characters/NPCs/Cartel Leader.docx` (+ `Small Cartel re-write`) · *(sparse — mostly direction)*
**Role:** Part 2 boss · **Faction:** head of [the Cartel](../../world/factions.md#the-cartel) 🏷

The apparent head of the Cartel — **"too smart for his own good,"** who thinks he understands enough of the situation to **turn on [Ouroboros](../../world/factions.md#ouroboros)**. Flagged in the source simply as a **"good boss fight."**

In the more detailed `Small Cartel re-write`, everyone (including Torv) believes he's the Cartel's true head, but he's actually **semi-led by an Ouroboros agent** who taught him to make fake stones in exchange for obedience and supplies. When he comes across a **powerful alkahest-bearing stone**, **greed** makes him hide it from his handler. Once Ouroboros learns of the stone — a real clue toward the true Stone — they decide the Cartel has **outlived its usefulness** and move to kill him and take it. This is also what stirs **the Philosopher's** interest (his Acolytes want the stone *and* the Leader alive, to learn about Ouroboros).

> 🔴 His death scene is the crux of an unresolved fork — who intervenes, in what order, and how the party is then turned against by the next faction. The candidate choreography (a second boss fight + cave-collapse escape; the Ouroboros agent assassinating him in the confusion to escape with the stone) is laid out in **[Q1](../../appendix/open-questions.md#q1-ouroboros-the-acolytes-and-the-cartel)**.
