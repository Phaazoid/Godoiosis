# Noemie

🟡 **Draft canon** · `Story/Characters/Playable/Noemie.docx` · *(spelled "Noemi" in some plot docs)*
**Role:** swordsman

A young female swordsman the party meets during the raid on the Academy. **[Torv](torv.md) sent her** to represent the Bleeding Hearts in the raid — which had been organized by mysterious clients who unsettled him. When she realizes the true nature of the job and sees [Rebecca](rebecca.md)'s dismemberment, she **turns on her own side** and takes it on herself to lead Rebecca, [Isaac](isaac.md), and [Aldin](aldin.md) to the Bleeding Hearts, where Rebecca can be fitted with a prosthetic arm.

Noemie is **headstrong and confident**, with an acute sense of **chivalry and fair play**. She feels personally responsible when others suffer and is committed to helping those caught in war's unintended consequences — a conviction that carries a streak of **naiveté**, which may temper as her arc progresses.

**If Noemie had the Philosopher's Stone:** she would **destroy it** — she believes no one person should wield that kind of power.
