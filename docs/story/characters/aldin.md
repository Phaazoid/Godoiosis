# Aldin

🟡 **Draft canon** · `Story/Characters/Playable/Aldin.docx` · *(spelling varies in source: **Aldin** / Aldwin / "Alin" — Aldin adopted as canonical)*
**Role:** healer · **Affinity:** **aether** (healing)

The alchemist healer who joins [Isaac](isaac.md) and [Rebecca](rebecca.md) after the raid on the Academy. As an **aether alchemist** he is initially useless in direct combat, but his role as a **healer is invaluable**.

Aldin is **meek and low on self-confidence, but strong-willed and compulsive**. He loves animals of every kind, with an intense fixation on **dragons**. He's secretly infatuated with Rebecca but sees her as far out of his league — which drives him to **insane acts of gallantry** that put him in mortal danger. These escalate across the game until they climax in his attempt to **command a dragon**, rounding out his arc. (He's oblivious to [Haima](haima.md)'s crush on him.)

**If Aldin had the Philosopher's Stone:** he'd be **too afraid to use it** — though sorely tempted by the prospect of **creating a dragon.**
