# Said

🟡 **Draft canon** · `Story/Characters/Playable/Said.docx`
**Role:** mechanist · **Weapon:** carbine · **Origin:** the [Sultanate](../world/factions.md#the-sultanate) · **Married to** [Sara](sara.md)

A **carbine-wielding mechanist** from the Sultanate (the same nation as [Ali](ali.md)). Together with his wife **[Sara](sara.md)**, he came to Paracelsus seeking adventure, having gotten hooked on thrilling journeys during a pilgrimage to a faraway land. He joins [Torv](torv.md) after his convoy is raided, expecting lots of **action and honor**.

He and Sara trade **verbal repartee** as they fight. He'll lightheartedly taunt his other allies too, but they rarely give him the satisfaction of worthy banter.

**If Said had the Philosopher's Stone:** he would **split it with his wife** and have them both live forever.
