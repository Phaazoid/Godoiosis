# Iosis — Story Wiki

The narrative canon for Iosis, distilled from the exported Google Drive wiki (`Story/**`) on **2026-06-26**.
This is the **Milestone-B story pass** that the [wiki triage](../design/wiki-triage.md) deferred.

> **Guiding law (dev, 2026-06-17):** *gameplay leads; story bends to fit gameplay, not vice-versa.* Lore may be **consulted** to ground gameplay (e.g. "what is a rune?"), but a story beat never overrides a design decision.

## How this was built

Everything here is a **faithful import** of what was already written — lightly cleaned, de-duplicated, and cross-linked, **not** rewritten or invented. Where two source docs disagreed, or a newer doc reframed an older one, **nothing was silently resolved**: the competing versions are parked side-by-side in [appendix/open-questions.md](appendix/open-questions.md) for the co-dev discussion. The originals remain untouched in `C:\Iosis\Drive Wiki\Story`.
