## Parts 2–4

Only high-level beats exist so far — see [plot-overview.md](plot-overview.md). Notable level *concepts* the docs call out (full brainstorm in [locations/battle-locales.md](locations/battle-locales.md)):

- **Part 2:** hunt-the-Cartel missions, a possible **train fight**, and the **underground Cartel stronghold → cave-collapse escape** (earth alchemy clears routes; checkpoint after the Cartel Leader to avoid frustration).
- **Part 3:** **dragon facilities in the wilderness**; a large **dragon battle** vs. Torv's Sister, possibly with CPF **mechs** joining.
- **Part 4:** the **CPF capital / cyborg King**, the **Paracelsus capital** set-pieces (the **Library "avec lazorz,"** runestone amplifiers, the dammed **river**, the **rune mine** under the city), and the **Philosopher's Keep**.
